Our Problem: Properly Introduced

The problem that we are trying to solve is noisy aircons. Noisy aircons can be annoying, especially during backyard events like barbecues or Thanksgiving dinners. We have come up with a solution and how it works.

Our Solution and How it Works

Our solution is using wheat straw and paint. We will use the paint to glue straw together, so we can create a denser barrier to make our glued straw soundproof. Straw is pretty soundproof, and easy to get. this is why we chose it.
